Our Services

At BekTier, we provide a range of IT and consultancy services to help your business achieve its goals. Whether you’re looking for a one-page site, a full multi-page website, or an in-depth analysis, we’re here to support you with customized solutions.

One-Pagers

A streamlined, single-page website that captures your core message effectively. Ideal for startups, small businesses, or landing pages for specific campaigns.

Multi-Pagers

Comprehensive multi-page websites that allow you to showcase multiple aspects of your business, services, and products in an organized, professional manner.

Wireframes

Blueprints of your website or application, providing a visual representation of the layout and structure before development begins. This helps to clarify the design and functionality early on.

Proofs of Concept (PoCs)

Small-scale models of your project to demonstrate feasibility. PoCs allow you to test ideas quickly and cost-effectively before full development.

Minimum Viable Products (MVPs)

Developed versions of your project that include only the essential features, allowing you to validate your idea in the market and gain user feedback for further improvements.

In-Depth Analyses

Detailed analyses of your current systems, processes, or market landscape to identify areas for improvement and opportunities for growth.

Consultancy and Advice

Expert advice and strategic planning tailored to your business needs. Our consultants work with you to identify the best approaches to achieve your goals.

Customized Solutions

Unique solutions developed to meet the specific requirements of your business. From concept to completion, we deliver high-quality results that align with your objectives.
